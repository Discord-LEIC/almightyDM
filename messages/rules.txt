RULES (read carefully)
0. Be respectful and mindful of others, both in tone and in content. To keep things running smoothly, keep your discussions within the appropriate channels and don’t overstep personal boundaries, especially when conflict may arise. 

1. Change your nickname to something others can recognize as you (e.g <firstname> <lastname>)

2. Use course channel mentions (#<course name>) instead of general ones (@everyone/@here/…). If you need Staff to help with something, @staff or a DM will do (don’t spam this).

3. If there are non-portuguese speakers in a channel, please use english.

4. Respect the Staff and their decisions. A lot of situations will be outside the scope of these rules and so they will be constantly dealing with all sorts of complicated situations.

5. Enjoy your stay! :)

1. Muda o teu nome para algo reconhecível (<primeiro> <último>)

5. Enjoy your stay! :)
